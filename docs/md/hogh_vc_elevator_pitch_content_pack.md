## Slide 3 — The Solution (Second Brain = Team of Agents)

### Title & subtitle
- **Title:** **Your Second Brain Isn’t a Bot—It’s a Bench of Specialists**
- **Subtitle:** *Under **TalentAgent™** and **CareerAgent™**, **teams of agents** run research, matching, outreach, screening, scheduling, and prep—then **hand off** to humans for interview, judgment, and the offer conversation. **NegotiationCoach™** whispers live, **privately**, on each side.*
- **Footer:** *Human Handoff Protocol™ — the Second Brain knows exactly when to go quiet*

### Data points
- **Second Brain (recruiter side — TalentAgent™):** orchestrates **RadarAgent**, **IntelligenceAgent**, **OutreachAgent**, **ScreenAgent**, **SchedulerAgent**, **OfferAgent**, **RetentionAgent**—one **persistent** system that remembers the requisition, the pipeline, and the evidence (`product_note.md` / `submission.md`).
- **Second Brain (candidate side — CareerAgent™):** orchestrates **Discovery**, **Application**, **SkillMap**, **OpportunityIntelligence**, **InterviewCoach**, **NegotiationAdvisor**, **CareerPath**—same persistence for **your** goals and story (`submission.md`).
- **Intelligence Briefs™:** **Candidate Intelligence Brief™** (recruiter) and **Opportunity Intelligence Brief™** (candidate)—narrative + **provenance** + verification flags, not a raw dump.
- **NegotiationCoach™:** **simultaneous private** guidance—**no** agent-to-agent negotiation; **no** auto-offers; **explicit acknowledgment** before activation (`product_note.md`).
- **Human Handoff Protocol™:** shortlist, outreach, offers = **human approval**; interview = **fully human** (`submission.md`).
- Category validation *(research, not HOGH-specific)*: **30–45%** time-to-fill reduction in pilots; **2×** outreach response vs templates; **40%** scheduling friction reduction.

### Slide composition
- **Layout:** **Two columns**—**Recruiter Second Brain** | **Candidate Second Brain**—each column shows a **cluster of small agent chips** (squad) rolling up into one **TalentAgent™** / **CareerAgent™** “brain” master mark—not three equal pillars.
- **Visuals:** Horizontal **journey** beneath: Intelligence → Match/outreach → **Briefs** → **Human interview (spotlight)** → Offer (**NegotiationCoach** as two private panels).
- **Optional:** Blurred miniature of a **brief**—tangible output of the Second Brain.

### Presenter talking points
1. “We’re not shipping **a chatbot**—we’re shipping a **Second Brain** implemented as a **coordinated team**: each agent owns a job; the orchestrator owns the outcome.”
2. “Recruiters stop winging interviews; candidates stop guessing culture—both walk in with a **brief** that only a **machine-scale research org** could produce in time.”
3. “At the offer, **NegotiationCoach™** is still **Second Brain** mode—**private** to each side—because that’s another **human** conversation, just **wildly under-informed** today.”

### Expected Q&A
| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| “Why so many agents?” | **Specialists beat monoliths** for quality, cost routing, and **governance**—you can test, gate, and audit **each** capability. |
| “Ethics of data?” | **Public** footprint + **official APIs** where required; **transparency** and correction paths for candidates (`product_note.md`). |
| “Who is customer #1?” | **Enterprise** (**TalentAgent™** Second Brain) seeds hiring velocity; **CareerAgent™** scales via partners once signal exists. |
