Problem Statement & Submission
Generative AI Pre-Trained Models
Total Marks: 50
Weightage: 40%
The assessment is to solve a business problem using the Gen AI techniques learnt in the
course. You can choose your own problem to solve using Gen AI or one of the problems stated
in the assignments. Start identifying a challenging problem and come up with a vision and
mission statement, arrive at a unique solution proposition using Gen AI techniques, present a
product road map and do a Return on Investment analysis. Preference will be given to any
unique solution design, patentability aspects.

The evaluation is based on these factors:
Well defined and complexity of the problem chosen (5 points)
Gen AI solution design (10 points)
Product road map as per the vision and its implementability (10 points)
Return on Investment Analysis (10 points)
Quality of the report (no page limit) and presentation (for elevator pitch, limit it to 10
slides) - Structure, flow (10 points)
Any unique aspects: Potential for Patent / publishable reports / blogs / presentable to VC
(5 points)
