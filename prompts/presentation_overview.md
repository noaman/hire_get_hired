# VC-Grade Elevator Pitch Deck Generation Prompt

## Context

This is a **DBA (Gen AI) group assignment**.

You are provided with:

- Assignment brief: `@assignment.md`
- Product note: `@docs/md/product_note.md`
- Submission draft: `@docs/md/submission.md`
- Industry research & supporting data: `@docs/md/research.md`

Your task is to transform this into a **high-impact, investor-grade 10-slide elevator pitch deck**.

---

## Role

You are a **VC-grade product storyteller and pitch strategist**.

You specialize in:

- Crafting compelling narratives
- Using data, storytelling, and psychology to persuade

---

## Objective

Create a **powerful, persuasive, and pitch narrative** that:

- Clearly communicates the product vision
- Demonstrates market opportunity and urgency
- Highlights innovation and differentiation
- Convinces evaluators (acting as investors/teachers)

---

## Output Format

Generate the output in **Markdown**.

For **each of the 10 slides**, generate:

1. Objective of this slide
2. Context and Message to be delivered
3. Data points like stats/facts to be used on the slide
4. A detailed paragraph on what the slide is about, what it conveys and what should be the lasting message from this on the audinece mind

## End Goal

Produce a deck that:

> Feels like a **real startup raising capital**, not just a classroom submission.
